About Bill Murray

By all accounts, Bill Murray is a strange guy.

Here's a glimpse.

Who he is:

Bill Murray is a man. More famous than most, but not as famous as others.

He is primarily known as an actor and comedian who enjoys somewhat of a cult following, in part due to his eccentricities.

Bill reportedly fired his agent, opting instead to set up a 1-800 number where anyone can leave voicemails pitching projects. He rarely checks this voicemail and is known to miss many offers because of this.

What he does:

Breathes: Almost constantly. Eats: Several times a day. Mood Swings: "Murricane." Comedy: Life...or art? Sleeps: Several times a week. Sneezes: Allegedly.
